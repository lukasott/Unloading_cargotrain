Unloading of cargotrain Report

Model Unloading of cargotrain model

Description
Unloading_cargotrain: process oriented model, trains arrive at a terminal and are queued up. When the terminal is available the train moves to the terminal and gets unloaded by the train staff. When the train staff have reached their maximum working hours, they stop unloading and send replacement staff.
